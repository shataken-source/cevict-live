### **Step 1: Shelter Registration**

1. Go to: `/petreunion/shelter/login`
2. Click: "Register Shelter"
3. Fill out:
   - Shelter Name (e.g., "ABC Animal Shelter")
   - Email
   - Password
   - Phone (optional)
   - Address (optional)

**Done!** Your shelter account is created.

---

### **Step 2: Login**

1. Go to: `/petreunion/shelter/login`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click: "Login"

**You're in!** You'll see your shelter dashboard.

---

### **Step 3: Quick Entry (When Pet Comes In)**

1. Click: **"Quick Entry (New Pet)"** button
2. Fill out the form:
   - Pet info (name, type, breed, color)
   - When/where lost
   - Details (markings, description)
   - Contact info
3. Upload photo (optional)
4. Click: **"Submit & Start Search"**

**Done!** Pet is now in the system and visible to the public.

---

### **Step 4: Manage Pets (When Pet Goes Out)**

On your dashboard, you'll see all your pets with buttons:

- **"Mark Found"** - Pet was found but not yet reunited
- **"Reunited"** - Pet was reunited with owner
- **"View"** - See the public page for this pet

**That's it!** Just click the button to update status.
