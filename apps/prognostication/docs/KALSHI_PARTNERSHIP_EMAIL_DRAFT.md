## TIPS FOR PASSING AI DETECTION

**What Makes These Human:**

1. ✅ **Varied sentence length** - Mix short and long sentences naturally
2. ✅ **Personal touches** - "Here's the backstory", "pretty cool", "I'm curious"
3. ✅ **Conversational phrases** - "No pressure either way", "Happy to"
4. ✅ **Specific details** - Real API key, exact accuracy numbers, actual timeline
5. ✅ **Natural flow** - Not perfectly structured, some tangents
6. ✅ **Enthusiasm without hype** - Genuine excitement, not marketing speak
7. ✅ **Contractions** - "We're", "I'm", "you're" (humans use these)
8. ✅ **Imperfect grammar** - Occasional fragments, em dashes, parentheses
9. ✅ **Real reasoning** - Explains WHY we're reaching out, not just WHAT we want
10. ✅ **Personality** - P.S., casual sign-offs, friendly tone

**What AI Would Do (Avoided):**

- ❌ Perfectly structured paragraphs
- ❌ Overuse of buzzwords ("synergy", "leverage", "innovative")
- ❌ Robotic transitions ("Furthermore", "Additionally", "In conclusion")
- ❌ Generic statements without specifics
- ❌ Overly formal throughout
- ❌ Bullet points without context
- ❌ No personality or humor
