# SmokersRights.com - Legal Navigator Strategy

## Core Identity
**"The Legal Navigator for Adult Tobacco Rights"**

Tagline: *"Know your rights. Navigate the laws. Travel with confidence."*

## Positioning
- **Not** a pro-smoking advocacy site
- **Not** a lifestyle/culture brand
- **IS** a practical, authoritative legal reference tool
- **IS** the go-to resource for "where can I legally smoke/vape?"

## Design Principles

### Visual Identity
- **Clean, trustworthy, data-driven**
- **Not** noir/detective (too gimmicky)
- **Not** rebellious/edgy (not advocacy)
- **IS** professional, authoritative, accessible

### Color Palette
- Primary: Deep blue (trust, authority)
- Secondary: Slate gray (professional)
- Accent: Green (legal, go)
- Warning: Orange (restrictions, penalties)

### Typography
- Headers: Bold, clear, sans-serif
- Body: Readable, serif or sans-serif
- Legal text: Monospace (for code/statutes)

---

## Content Voice & Tone

### Voice
- **Authoritative** but not preachy
- **Informative** but not dry
- **Practical** but not condescending
- **Neutral** but helpful

### Tone Examples

✅ **Good:**
"California prohibits smoking in all enclosed workplaces, including bars and restaurants. Violations may result in fines up to $100 for individuals."

❌ **Bad:**
"California's nanny state has banned smoking everywhere! Fight back against government overreach!"
