# PetReunion Chatbot - Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

**Q: How do I report a lost pet?**
A: Visit our website and click "Report Lost Pet". You'll need photos, your pet's description, and where they were last seen. The process takes about 5 minutes.

**Q: How do I report a found pet?**
A: Click "Report Found Pet" on our site. Upload photos and describe where you found them. We'll help match them with lost pet reports.

**Q: Is PetReunion free?**
A: Yes! Our service is completely free. We're community-powered and believe reuniting pets shouldn't cost money.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: It's helpful but not required. Creating an account lets you track your reports and get updates.

## Using the Platform

**Q: How do I search for a lost pet?**
A: Use our search page. You can filter by location, breed, color, size, and more. The more details you provide, the better matches you'll get.

**Q: Can I search by photo?**
A: Yes! Our image matching feature can help identify pets from photos. Upload a photo and we'll search for similar matches.

**Q: How long does it take to find a match?**
A: It varies. Some pets are found within hours, others take days or weeks. The key is getting your report out quickly and keeping it active.

**Q: Can I edit my report after posting?**
A: Yes! You can update photos, location, contact info, and other details anytime. Keeping your report current helps with matching.

**Q: What if I find my pet?**
A: Great news! Please mark your report as "Found" so others know the search is complete. You can also share your success story to give others hope.

## Privacy & Safety

**Q: Who can see my contact information?**
A: Your contact info is private. Only users who match with your report can see it, and only if you choose to contact them.

**Q: Is it safe to meet someone to return a pet?**
A: We recommend meeting in public places and verifying ownership. Ask for photos or vet records. Trust your instincts and involve authorities if needed.

**Q: What if someone contacts me about my lost pet?**
A: Verify it's your pet by asking for recent photos or details only you would know. Be cautious of scams - never send money before verifying.

**Q: Can I report suspicious activity?**
A: Yes, please report any abuse, spam, or suspicious behavior. We take safety seriously.

## Technical Questions

**Q: What types of pets can I report?**
A: Dogs, cats, birds, small animals, reptiles, and other pets. We help with all types of companion animals.

**Q: Do you work with animal shelters?**
A: Yes! We partner with local shelters and have a directory to help you find nearby resources.

**Q: How does image matching work?**
A: Our AI analyzes photos to find similar pets. It's a helpful tool, but always verify matches with other details like location and description.

**Q: Can I get email alerts?**
A: Yes, you can opt in for email notifications about new matches or updates to your reports.

**Q: Does PetReunion work on mobile?**
A: Yes! Our site is mobile-friendly and works on all devices.

## Success & Support

**Q: How successful is PetReunion?**
A: Many pets are successfully reunited through our platform. Success depends on quick reporting, good photos, and community sharing.

**Q: What should I do if my pet has been missing for weeks?**
A: Don't give up! Many pets are found weeks or even months later. Keep your report active, check shelters regularly, and continue sharing.

**Q: Can I get help if I'm feeling overwhelmed?**
A: Losing a pet is incredibly stressful. We're here to help guide you through the process. Take it one step at a time.

**Q: How can I help others find their pets?**
A: Share reports on social media, check found pet reports, and keep an eye out in your neighborhood. Every share helps!

**Q: What if I need to talk to a real person?**
A: For urgent issues or emotional support, we can connect you with our team. Just ask!

## Location & Coverage

**Q: What areas does PetReunion cover?**
A: We're available nationwide (or specify your coverage area). Users can report pets from anywhere.

**Q: Can I search in multiple cities?**
A: Yes, you can search by location and expand your search radius to cover multiple areas.

**Q: What if my pet was lost while traveling?**
A: Report the location where they were lost. You can also search in that area and your home area.

## Reporting Tips

**Q: What makes a good lost pet report?**
A: Clear photos from multiple angles, detailed description, accurate location, and keeping the report updated.

**Q: How many photos should I upload?**
A: At least 2-3 photos showing different angles. Full body shots and close-ups of distinctive features are most helpful.

**Q: What if I don't know my pet's exact breed?**
A: That's okay! Describe what they look like - size, color, coat type. Our search works with descriptions too.

**Q: Should I include my pet's name?**
A: Yes, if they respond to it. But also include other identifying features since names can change.
