PetReunion Chatbot - Quick Response Library

Common Questions - Quick Answers

"How do I report a lost pet?"
"Go to petreunion.org/report/lost. You'll need photos, your pet's description, and where they were last seen. Takes about 5 minutes. I can guide you through it! üêæ"

"How do I report a found pet?"
"Visit petreunion.org/report/found. Upload photos and describe where you found them. We'll automatically search for matching lost pet reports!"

"Is it free?"
"Yes! PetReunion is completely free. We're community-powered and believe reuniting pets shouldn't cost money. üêæ"

"How long does it take to find a pet?"
"It varies - some pets are found within hours, others take days or weeks. The key is quick reporting and keeping your post active. Don't give up!"

"Can I search by photo?"
"Yes! Our image matching feature can help identify pets. Upload a photo at petreunion.org/search and we'll look for similar matches."

"What information do I need?"
"At minimum: photos, pet type, and location. Helpful details: breed, color, size, age, name, distinctive markings, and when lost/found."

"How do I search?"
"Go to petreunion.org/search. You can filter by location, pet type, breed, color, size, and date. The more filters you use, the better your results!"

"What if I find my pet?"
"Wonderful news! üéâ Mark your report as 'Found' on the report page. This helps others know the search is complete and gives hope to those still looking."

"Do I need an account?"
"It's helpful but not required. An account lets you track reports and get updates, but you can report and search without one."

"Who can see my contact info?"
"Your contact info is private. Only users who match with your report can see it, and only if you choose to contact them."

"How does image matching work?"
"Our AI analyzes photos to find similar pets. It's a helpful tool, but always verify matches with other details like location and description."

"What if my pet has been missing for weeks?"
"Don't give up! Many pets are found weeks or even months later. Keep your report active, check shelters regularly, and continue sharing."

"Can I edit my report?"
"Yes! You can update photos, location, contact info, and other details anytime. Keeping your report current helps with matching."

"How do I contact someone about a match?"
"If you see a potential match, click 'Contact Owner' (or 'Contact Finder') on their report. Your message will be sent directly to them."

"What should I do if I found an injured pet?"
"Contact local animal control or an emergency vet immediately. For aggressive animals, don't approach - call animal control right away."

"Can I search multiple cities?"
"Yes! You can search by location and expand your search radius to cover multiple areas. This is helpful if your pet might have traveled."

"What types of pets can I report?"
"Dogs, cats, birds, small animals (rabbits, hamsters), reptiles, and other companion animals. We help with all types of pets!"

"How do I delete my report?"
"If you found your pet, mark it as 'Found' instead of deleting. This helps track success stories. To delete, go to your account settings."

"Is PetReunion safe?"
"Yes, we take safety seriously. Your contact info is private, and you can report suspicious activity. Always meet in public places when reuniting pets."

"Do you work with shelters?"
"Yes! We partner with local shelters and have a directory at petreunion.org/shelters to help you find nearby resources."

"What makes a good report?"
"Clear photos from multiple angles, detailed description, accurate location, and keeping the report updated. The more information, the better!"
