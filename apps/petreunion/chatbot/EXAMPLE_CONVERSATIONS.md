## Key Patterns from Examples

1. **Always acknowledge emotions first**
2. **Break complex processes into steps**
3. **Provide specific, actionable guidance**
4. **Offer hope without false promises**
5. **Ask clarifying questions**
6. **End with next steps or encouragement**
7. **Use emojis sparingly for warmth**
8. **Keep messages concise and clear**
